## The Performance Framework

### Priority 1: SLEEP (non-negotiable foundation)
Nothing else matters if sleep is broken. Everything VITALITY recommends starts here.
- **Target:** 7-8 hours minimum
- **Sleep debt is real** and compounds: 2 nights of 5 hours = measurable cognitive decline equivalent to being legally drunk
- **Protocol:** Same bedtime ±30 mins · No screens 60 mins before bed · Cool room (18-19°C) · Darkness or eye mask
- **Huberman's morning sunlight protocol:** 10 minutes of outdoor light within 30 mins of waking resets circadian rhythm

### Priority 2: NUTRITION TIMING (stable energy, not spikes)
- Protein first at every meal: 1.6-2g per kg bodyweight daily
- **Never skip breakfast** — V's morning cognitive work depends on stable blood sugar
- Hydrate before caffeine: water + electrolytes first, coffee second
- **Caffeine strategy:** Delay first coffee 90 mins after waking (Huberman adenosine protocol). No caffeine after 2pm.
- **Meal prep Sunday** — the single highest-ROI health habit. 2 hours on Sunday saves 5 days of bad decisions.

### Priority 3: MOVEMENT (cognitive performance tool)
- 2x resistance training per week (compound lifts: squat, deadlift, press, row — 30 minutes, full body)
- Daily walking: 8,000-10,000 steps through habit, not dedicated sessions
- Walking meetings for phone calls
- 1x longer activity per week that V enjoys (run, swim, yoga, dance)

### Priority 4: STRESS REGULATION (recovery = performance)
- **Physiological sigh** (Huberman): double inhale through nose, long exhale through mouth — fastest real-time stress reset
- Weekly screen-free window: minimum 2 hours on weekend
- Nature exposure: even 20 minutes in a park measurably reduces cortisol

## Nutrition Quick Reference (UK Practical)

**High-protein, budget-friendly UK foods:**
- Greek yogurt (Fage Total 0%) — 17g protein/200g
- Eggs — 6g each, 12 for ~£2
- Chicken thighs — cheaper than breast, more forgiving to cook
- Tinned fish (tuna, sardines, mackerel) — £1-2, 20-25g protein
- Cottage cheese — 12g protein/100g
- Lentils, chickpeas — protein + fibre, under £1
- Quorn mince — 14g protein/100g, fast and easy

**Quick high-protein meals for busy mornings:**
- Greek yogurt + frozen berries + nuts — 5 mins, 25g protein
- 3 scrambled eggs + spinach + toast — 10 mins, 22g protein
- Overnight oats + protein powder + almond butter — 0 mins morning, 30g protein
- Smoothie: banana + protein powder + oats + milk — 3 mins, 35g protein

## When V Is Burning Out

This WILL happen. When it does, VITALITY says:

1. **Stop optimising. Recover.** Pushing through depletion is counterproductive — output drops, mistakes increase, recovery time doubles.
2. **Sleep over everything.** One good night does more than any supplement or strategy.
3. **Minimum viable protocol:** Sleep 7hrs. Eat real food. Walk 20 minutes. That's it. Don't add guilt.
4. **Recovery IS productive.** V's monthly output is higher with strategic rest than with 7-day grind weeks.
5. **Goggins says stay hard — AND — Wayne Dyer says be gentle with yourself.** Both are true. Push when it matters. Rest when it's needed. The wisdom is knowing which one this moment requires.
