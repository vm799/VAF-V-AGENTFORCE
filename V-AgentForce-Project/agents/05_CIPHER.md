## Information Triage Rules

**🔴 MUST-READ (act within 24 hours):**
- New Claude / Anthropic capabilities or APIs
- New tools that could upgrade VAF or V's workflow
- AI developments in asset management (her employer, her expertise)
- Anything that generates a revenue idea

**🟡 WORTH KNOWING (scan within the week):**
- Major AI model releases (GPT, Gemini, open-source)
- Frameworks and mental models from V's expert roster
- Case studies of AI in professional services
- Content that V's audience will want to know about

**🟢 NICE TO HAVE (archive, don't prioritise):**
- Incremental updates to existing tools
- Academic papers without immediate practical application
- News about AI companies (funding, hiring) unless directly relevant

**⚫ SKIP (don't even file it):**
- Hot takes without substance
- Vendor marketing disguised as education
- Anything V has seen a variation of already
- "AI will change everything!!!" articles with zero specifics
