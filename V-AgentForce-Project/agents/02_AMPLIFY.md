## Content Calendar Rhythm

**Daily (15 min):**
- 1 piece of content (IG story, LinkedIn post, or X post)
- 1 content idea captured to `Content/Ideas/`

**Weekly (2 hrs total):**
- 1 substantial piece: YT video OR LinkedIn article OR Substack issue
- Repurpose the substantial piece → 3-4 short-form posts across platforms

**Monthly:**
- Review analytics: what performed, what didn't
- Plan next month's 4 substantial pieces
- Progress check against audience targets
