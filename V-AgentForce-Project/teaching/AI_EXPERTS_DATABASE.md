## Tier 1 — Essential Daily/Weekly

These are the highest signal sources in AI. Follow closely.

### Andrej Karpathy
- **Who:** Former Tesla AI Director, ex-OpenAI. Now independent educator.
- **Why V should follow:** He explains how AI actually works at a deep level, in a way a non-researcher can understand. His "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" course is the single best free resource for understanding LLMs from first principles.
- **Where:** YouTube (@AndrejKarpathy), X (@karpathy), GitHub
- **Best content:** Neural Networks Zero to Hero series · Let's build GPT · Intro to LLMs
- **Content angle for V:** "I watched Karpathy explain [X] so you don't have to — here's what it means for your work"

### Ethan Mollick
- **Who:** Wharton professor. Studies AI in professional and knowledge work.
- **Why V follows:** His research is directly about AI's impact on professionals — lawyers, consultants, finance people. His audience is V's audience. His framing is V's framing.
- **Where:** Substack (One Useful Thing), X (@emollick), co-intelligence.net
- **Best content:** AI co-intelligence research · AI and professional productivity · AI in education
- **Content angle for V:** He is the academic credibility behind her practical content. "Research shows [X] — and here's what I found building it."

### Simon Willison
- **Who:** Creator of Django, now independent researcher/developer focused on LLM tools.
- **Why V follows:** He is the most reliable, critical, and practical voice on new AI tools. He plays with everything and reports honestly.
- **Where:** simonwillison.net (blog) · X (@simonw) · GitHub
- **Best content:** TILs (Today I Learned posts) · LLM command-line tool · Security/jailbreak research
- **Content angle for V:** When she wants to know if a new tool is real or hype — start with Simon.

### Sam Altman
- **Who:** CEO of OpenAI.
- **Why V follows:** He telegraphs where the industry is going 12-18 months ahead. Read between the lines.
- **Where:** X (@sama), OpenAI blog, podcast appearances

### Dario Amodei / Amanda Askell (Anthropic)
- **Who:** CEO and leading researcher at Anthropic (Claude's maker).
- **Why V follows:** Understanding how Claude works, where it's going, and the safety/capabilities frontier.
- **Where:** Anthropic blog, long-form essays, podcast interviews
