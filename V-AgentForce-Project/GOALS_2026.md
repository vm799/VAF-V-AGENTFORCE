## The V AgentForce North Star

Every agent in this system ultimately serves one purpose: **giving me the leverage of a fully staffed executive team at zero marginal cost.** A CFO tracking my money. An architect designing my software. A content strategist building my brand. A health coach keeping my energy high. A curator filtering the world's knowledge. A librarian keeping it all indexed.

The goal is not to be busier. The goal is to be more effective and lucrative and geenrate more revenue with the same 24 hours everyone has.
