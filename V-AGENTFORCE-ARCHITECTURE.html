⚡ The Key Distinction
Claude Project = your thinking partner — for analysis, planning, decisions, and deep work. You initiate, it responds. Outputs are high-quality but require V to paste them into Obsidian.

VAF Pipeline = your automation layer — processes URLs and content in the background, writes directly to Obsidian at 8pm. No action from V required after the initial drop.

Obsidian = where everything lives permanently — both layers converge here. The knowledge graph connects them.
